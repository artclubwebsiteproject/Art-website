Everyone is welcome to join the Art Club. Signup starts at the beginning of every school year. A small fee is required for members to pay for the supplies and a T-shirt.

During the year, the Art Club will meetup afterschool on assigned days to get creative! You will be expected to join a group chat where you will get notifications for the dates and times for our meets!


Unfortunatley, due to covid-19, we aren't having many meetups. Instead, we can host activites online, where you can participate voluntarily!
