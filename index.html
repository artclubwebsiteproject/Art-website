Scotts Hill High School Art Club

Creative individuals who enjoy art activities, You've come to the right place!

Welcome to the SHHS art club. Here at SHHS, we take pride in providing our creative students with the supplies they need to bring their ideas to life! Students will participate in club activities, each expanding their skills as they work. We're thankful for our members and team leaders, who will be there for you if you need tips or advice. We just know you'll love expressing yourself and putting your work out there for other students of SHHS to see!
